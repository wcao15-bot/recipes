Lasagna Bowls

480 Cal | 44g Protein | 48g Carbs | 14g Fat

Ingredients

  • 907g (32oz) 93% lean ground beef
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) tomato paste
  • 840g (54 oz) crushed tomatoes (two 28 oz cans)
  • 960g (32oz) bone broth (two 16oz boxes)
  • 1 Tbsp onion powder, 1 Tbsp garlic powder, 1.5 Tbsp salt, 1 Tbsp Italian herbs, 1 tsp black pepper
  • 120g (½ cup) fat-free milk
  • 200g blended 2% cottage cheese
  • 120g (1 cup) Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 60g (½ cup) low-fat mozzarella
  • 448g (16 oz) oven-ready lasagna
  • 240g (1 cup) water
  • Toppings: 30g low-fat ricotta per bowl, fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Add the ground beef to the slow cooker. Add in the tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, bone broth, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, Italian herbs, and black pepper. Stir well, breaking up any large chunks of beef, until everything is fully combined. Cover and cook on high for 3-4 hours or low for 4+ hours until the beef is tender and flavors are fully melded.
  2. Once done, break up the oven ready lasagna sheets and add them to the slow cooker. Pour in 1 cup of water and mix thoroughly to ensure all pasta pieces are separated and submerged in the sauce. Cover and cook for an additional 20-30 minutes on high, or until the pasta is cooked through.
  3. When the pasta is tender, blend the 2% cottage cheese, shred the Parmigiano Reggiano and low-fat mozzarella. Add to the slow cooker with the fat-free milk. Stir until the cheese is melted and fully combined, creating a creamy sauce.
  4. Let the mixture sit, covered, for another 10 minutes to thicken slightly. Chop the basil and serve each portion with a topping of low-fat ricotta and basil.

Carnitas Inspired Pulled Pork Burritos

510 Cal | 35g Protein | 53g Carbs | 17g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1790g (64 oz) pork loin
  • 2 onions, diced & 1 Tbsp oil
  • 2 Tbsp salt, 1 Tbsp oregano, 2 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp cumin, 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • Juice from 2 oranges + peels, 6 cloves garlic
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) apple cider vinegar, 3 bay leaves, 40g (2 Tbsp) honey
  • Cornstarch slurry: 1 tsp cornstarch + 1 tsp water
  • Rice: 320g jasmine rice, 745g bone broth, 2 Tbsp tomato paste, 1 Tbsp oil, 3 cloves garlic
  • 15 tortillas & 30g Greek yogurt per burrito

Instructions

  1. Cut the pork loin into 4-5 large chunks and set aside. Dice the onions and cook in a skillet over medium-high heat with oil until browned and caramelized (8-10 mins).
  2. Place pork in slow cooker. Add onions, salt, pepper, oregano, cumin, cinnamon, garlic, honey, and vinegar. Squeeze orange juice over and add peels/bay leaves.
  3. Cook on high 4-5 hours or low 5-6 hours. Remove liquid to a pan and boil to reduce for 10-15 mins (use slurry if needed). Shred pork and pour reduced liquid back over.
  4. Rice: Fry minced garlic and tomato paste in oil for 3-5 mins. Add rice, broth, and seasonings. Simmer covered for 20-25 mins.
  5. Assemble across 15 tortillas with rice, pork, and yogurt.

Caramelized Onion Beef Burritos

535 Cal | 42g Protein | 54g Carbs | 16g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1340g (48oz) flat cut brisket or chuck eye roast
  • 45g (3 Tbsp) Worcestershire, 15g (1 Tbsp) Dijon mustard, 100g (1/2 cup) bone broth
  • 1 Tbsp salt, 1 Tbsp garlic powder, 2 tsp onion powder, 2 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp thyme, 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  • 4 yellow onions, 30g (2 Tbsp) butter, 960g (32oz) beef bone broth, 30g (2 Tbsp) Worcestershire
  • 240g (1 1/4 cups) rice, 300g (1 1/4 cups) 0% Greek yogurt, 60g (1/2 cup) cheddar, 1 tsp nutritional yeast
  • 11 tortillas, Cilantro/green onions

Instructions

  1. Remove excess fat from meat. Place in slow cooker with seasonings, broth, mustard, and Worcestershire. Cook high 4-5 hrs or low 5-6 hrs.
  2. Sauté diced onions on high until browning. Reduce to medium, add butter, and sauté 10-15 mins. Add 32oz broth and 2 Tbsp Worcestershire; boil until liquid evaporates.
  3. Shred beef in slow cooker. Add the caramelized onions and mix.
  4. Cook rice. Mix with yogurt, shredded cheddar, nutritional yeast, and cilantro/green onions. Season with salt and garlic powder.
  5. Assemble: Cheesy rice followed by beef/onion mix into 11 tortillas.

BBQ Beef Loaded Sweet Potatoes

555 Cal | 51g Protein | 52g Carbs | 17g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1790g (64oz) flat cut brisket or chuck eye roast
  • 300g (1 1/4 cups) Dr Pepper, 24g BBQ seasoning, 15ml Worcestershire, 30g no-sugar ketchup, 3 chipotles
  • Finishing Sauce: 40g honey, 25g apple cider vinegar, 10g Dijon, 30g ketchup, 120g (1/2 cup) Dr Pepper
  • 2000g sweet potatoes (~10 medium), Olive oil, salt
  • Toppings: 200g cheddar, 400g 0% Greek yogurt, green onions

Instructions

  1. Slow cook beef with seasonings and 1 1/4 cups Dr Pepper (4-5 hrs High / 6-8 hrs Low).
  2. Mix finishing sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Dice sweet potatoes. Roast at 400°F with oil and salt for 25-35 mins.
  4. Shred beef. Transfer liquid to a pan, add finishing sauce, and simmer 10 mins to reduce. Use cornstarch slurry if needed.
  5. Mix yogurt with a splash of milk and garlic powder.
  6. Assemble 10 bowls: sweet potatoes, beef, cheese, yogurt sauce, and green onions.

Cheesesteak Pasta

520 Cal | 48g Protein | 49g Carbs | 15g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1350g (48oz) brisket or chuck eye roast
  • 1 Tbsp each: salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder
  • 240g (8oz) beef bone broth
  • 4 red bell peppers, 4 poblanos, 4 jalapenos, 2 sweet onions
  • 800g 2% cottage cheese, 100g Parmigiano Reggiano, 150g provolone, 350ml 1% milk
  • 672g (24oz) pasta (dry weight)

Instructions

  1. Slow cook beef with seasonings and broth (4-5 hrs High / 5-6 hrs Low).
  2. Dice all peppers and onions. Roast at 450°F for 30-45 mins.
  3. Shred beef and stir in roasted veggies.
  4. Blend cottage cheese, parmesan, provolone, milk, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  5. Boil pasta for exactly half the time on the box; drain.
  6. Mix pasta and cheese sauce into slow cooker. Cook for 20-25 mins until pasta is tender and sauce thickens.

Queso Chicken & Rice

515 Cal | 48g Protein | 51g Carbs | 13g Fat

Ingredients

  • 24oz chicken breast, 1.5 tsp avocado oil, 1.5 packets taco seasoning
  • 300g basmati rice
  • Queso Sauce: 250g 2% Greek yogurt, 85g red enchilada sauce, 85g pico, 85g cheddar, 100g 1/3 fat cream cheese, 65g green chiles
  • Seasoning: 20g nutritional yeast, 10g brown sugar, 1/2 tsp garlic/onion powder, pinch of salt
  • Chopped Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice. Boil with 450g water, then simmer on low (covered) for 15 mins. Let sit (covered) for 10 mins.
  2. Flatten chicken. Rub with oil and coat heavily in taco seasoning. Dry brine for 45-60 mins.
  3. Air fry chicken at 375°F for 12 mins. Rest for 5 mins, then dice.
  4. In a pan on low: combine yogurt, enchilada sauce, pico, cheeses, chiles, and seasonings. Stir until melted (add milk if needed). Stir in cilantro.
  5. Serve chicken over rice, smothered in Queso sauce.

Chipotle Brisket Breakfast Burritos

505 Cal | 48g Protein | 40g Carbs | 17g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1340g (48 oz) brisket
  • Slow Cooker Mix: 200g chipotles, 200g red enchilada sauce, 40g honey, 15g apple cider vinegar, 1 Tbsp each salt/garlic/onion powder, 2 tsp pepper
  • Egg Base: 600g egg whites, 5 whole eggs, 350g cottage cheese, 2 tsp corn starch, 100g cheddar
  • Yogurt Sauce: 400g 0% Greek yogurt, 50g hot sauce, seasonings
  • 12 large tortillas, Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Trim fat from brisket. Place in slow cooker with seasonings, chipotles, sauce, honey, and vinegar. Cook High 4-5 hrs / Low 5-6 hrs.
  2. Shred beef. Reduce liquid in a pan for 10-15 mins, thicken with cornstarch slurry (2 Tbsp cornstarch/water), and mix back into beef.
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F. Blend egg whites, eggs, cottage cheese, and corn starch. Pour into a sheet pan, top with cheddar, and bake 20-30 mins. Slice into 12 rectangles.
  4. Mix yogurt sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  5. Assemble: 12 tortillas + sauce + egg rectangle + beef. Garnish with cilantro and wrap.

Creamy Chicken Fajita Burrito

390 Cal | 36g Protein | 34g Carbs | 12g Fat

Ingredients

  • 32oz thin sliced chicken breast
  • Marinade: 1/2 bunch cilantro, 1 lime (juice), 1 Tbsp oil, 1 Tbsp honey, 1 Tbsp garlic powder, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp cumin, 2 tsp salt, 1/3 cup water
  • 4 bell peppers, 1 sweet onion, salt
  • Mix-ins: 220g 0% Greek yogurt, 1 Tbsp chipotle hot sauce, 100g mozzarella
  • 10 tortillas & 300g refried ranchero beans

Instructions

  1. Combine marinade ingredients. Add chicken and marinate for 30+ mins.
  2. Dice peppers and onion. Roast at 400°F for 40 mins.
  3. Air fry chicken at 385°F for 9 mins.
  4. Dice chicken. Mix with roasted veggies, yogurt, hot sauce, and mozzarella.
  5. Spread 30g beans on each tortilla. Add chicken mix and wrap.

Pepperoni Pizza Bagel Bites

515 Cal | 30g Protein | 65g Carbs | 17g Fat

Ingredients

  • 10 English muffins
  • 400g Marinara, 50g black garlic sauce, 33g parmigiano reggiano
  • 60g chopped hot peppers, 200g 2% Mozzarella, 60 slices turkey pepperoni
  • 33g hot honey, Oregano, Basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. Split muffins, spray with oil, and bake for 5 mins until crispy.
  3. Top each half with marinara, peppers, mozzarella, and pepperoni.
  4. Drizzle with hot honey and bake for another 5 mins until bubbly.
  5. Garnish with parmesan, oregano, and basil. (Serving size = 4 bites).

Chicken Burrito Bowls

505 Cal | 45g Protein | 44g Carbs | 16g Fat

Ingredients

  • 24oz skinless chicken thighs + seasonings (salt, garlic, onion, smoked paprika, chili powder)
  • 300g Spanish RightRice
  • Guacamole: 200g Avocado, salt, pepper, garlic powder, 50g green enchilada sauce
  • Pico: 5 Roma tomatoes, 1 white onion, lime juice (2), cilantro, 1-2 jalapeños, seasonings
  • Toppings: 200g Greek yogurt, hot sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 375°F. Season chicken and air fry for 12-15 mins.
  2. Cook rice according to package.
  3. Mix Pico ingredients in a bowl. In another bowl, mash avocado with seasonings and enchilada sauce for guac.
  4. Dice chicken after resting 5 mins.
  5. Assemble 5 bowls: rice, chicken, pico, and guac. Top with 40g yogurt and hot sauce.
