Lasagna Bowls

Prep: 20 mins | Cook: 3–4 hours (High)
480 Cal | 44g Protein | 48g Carbs | 14g Fat

Ingredients

  • 907g (32oz) 93% lean ground beef
  • 30g (2 tbsp) tomato paste
  • 840g (54oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 960g (32oz) bone broth
  • 1 tbsp each: onion powder, garlic powder, Italian herbs
  • 1.5 tbsp salt, 1 tsp black pepper
  • 120g (½ cup) fat-free milk
  • 200g blended 2% cottage cheese
  • 120g (1 cup) Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 60g (½ cup) low-fat mozzarella
  • 448g (16oz) oven-ready lasagna sheets
  • 240g (1 cup) water
  • Toppings: low-fat ricotta, fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Add beef, tomato paste, tomatoes, broth, and seasonings to slow cooker. Cook High 3-4 hours.
  2. Break lasagna sheets into cooker with 1 cup water. Cook 20-30 mins on High.
  3. Stir in cottage cheese, parmesan, mozzarella, and milk.
  4. Sit 10 mins to thicken. Garnish with ricotta and basil.

Carnitas Inspired Pulled Pork Burritos

Prep: 30 mins | Cook: 4–5 hours (High)
510 Cal | 35g Protein | 53g Carbs | 17g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1790g (64oz) pork loin
  • 2 onions, diced & 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp salt, 1 tbsp oregano, 2 tsp pepper, 1 tsp cumin
  • Juice of 2 oranges, 6 cloves garlic
  • 15g apple cider vinegar, 3 bay leaves, 40g honey
  • 320g jasmine rice & 745g chicken bone broth
  • 15 tortillas & 30g Greek yogurt per burrito

Instructions

  1. Brown onions in oil. Slow cook pork with onions, spices, juice, garlic, honey, and vinegar (4-5 hrs High).
  2. Shred pork. Reduce liquid in pan for 10 mins, then pour back over meat.
  3. Sauté garlic/tomato paste, add rice/broth, and simmer 20 mins.
  4. Assemble tortillas with rice, pork, and yogurt.

Caramelized Onion Beef Burritos

Prep: 30 mins | Cook: 4–5 hours (High)
535 Cal | 42g Protein | 54g Carbs | 16g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1340g brisket or chuck eye roast
  • 45g Worcestershire, 15g Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp salt, 1 tbsp garlic powder, 2 tsp onion powder, 2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp thyme, 1/8 tsp nutmeg, 100g bone broth
  • 4 yellow onions & 30g butter
  • 240g rice, 300g Greek yogurt, 60g cheddar
  • 11 tortillas

Instructions

  1. Slow cook brisket with seasonings and broth until tender.
  2. Sauté sliced onions in butter for 15 mins until browned.
  3. Cook rice. Mix with yogurt and cheddar.
  4. Shred beef, mix with onions, and assemble in tortillas with rice.

BBQ Beef Loaded Sweet Potatoes

Prep: 20 mins | Cook: 4–5 hours (High)
555 Cal | 51g Protein | 52g Carbs | 17g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1790g brisket or chuck eye roast
  • 300g Dr Pepper, 24g BBQ seasoning
  • 15ml Worcestershire, 30g no-sugar ketchup, 3 chipotle peppers
  • 2000g sweet potatoes, 200g cheddar, 400g Greek yogurt

Instructions

  1. Slow cook beef with Dr Pepper and seasonings until tender.
  2. Dice and roast sweet potatoes at 400°F for 30 mins.
  3. Shred beef and reduce liquid. Top potatoes with beef, cheese, and yogurt.

Cheesesteak Pasta

Prep: 25 mins | Cook: 4–5 hours (High)
520 Cal | 48g Protein | 49g Carbs | 15g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1350g brisket, 1 tbsp each salt/pepper/garlic/onion powder, 240g broth
  • 4 red peppers, 4 poblanos, 4 jalapenos, 2 sweet onions
  • 800g cottage cheese, 100g Parmigiano, 150g provolone, 350ml 1% milk
  • 672g pasta

Instructions

  1. Slow cook beef with seasonings and broth until tender.
  2. Roast sliced peppers and onions at 450°F for 40 mins.
  3. Blend cottage cheese, parmesan, provolone, and milk.
  4. Mix beef, veggies, cooked pasta, and sauce. Cook Low 20 mins.

Queso Chicken & Rice

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 45 mins
515 Cal | 48g Protein | 51g Carbs | 13g Fat

Ingredients

  • 24oz chicken breast, 1.5 packets taco seasoning, 300g rice
  • 250g Greek yogurt, 85g enchilada sauce, 85g pico, 85g cheddar
  • 100g cream cheese, 65g green chiles

Instructions

  1. Cook rice. Air fry seasoned chicken at 375°F for 12 mins.
  2. Melt yogurt, sauce, pico, and cheeses in pan on low.
  3. Serve chicken over rice with queso sauce.

Creamy Chicken Fajita Burrito

Prep: 20 mins | Cook: 40 mins
390 Cal | 36g Protein | 34g Carbs | 12g Fat

Ingredients

  • 32oz chicken, cilantro, lime, honey, garlic powder
  • 4 bell peppers, 1 onion, 220g Greek yogurt, 100g mozzarella
  • 300g refried ranchero beans, 10 tortillas

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken 30 mins. Roast peppers/onions at 400°F for 40 mins.

515 Cal | 30g Protein | 65g Carbs | 17g Fat

Ingredients
